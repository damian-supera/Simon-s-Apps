2. Scope of this policy

This policy explains how data is processed in the Micro Workout Timer mobile app and in third-party services related to analytics, diagnostics, and advertising.

Micro Workout Timer does not require account creation or sign-in. The app can be used without registering a user account.

  • Data stored locally on the device, such as timer settings, app preferences, and other configuration needed for the app to function.
  • Technical and diagnostic data, such as operating system version, device model, app instance identifiers, crash logs, and stability-related information.
  • Analytics data processed through Firebase or Google Analytics for Firebase, such as app usage events, installation-related data, and basic device information.
  • Advertising-related data, if the app displays ads, such as advertising identifiers, basic device information, and data needed to display and measure ads through Google or advertising partners.

  • App functionality and local settings storage: Article 6(1)(b) or 6(1)(f) GDPR, depending on your operating model.
  • Security, diagnostics, technical statistics, and app quality improvement: Article 6(1)(f) GDPR.
  • Displaying and measuring ads and funding the free version of the app: Article 6(1)(f) GDPR or Article 6(1)(a) GDPR where consent is required.

7. Retention period

Data stored locally on the device remains there until the user clears the app data, removes it from the device, or uninstalls the app.

Data processed by external services such as Firebase, Google Analytics for Firebase, or advertising services may be retained according to those services' configuration, retention settings, and provider policies.

Because the app does not require a user account, the main way to remove local app data is to clear the app storage in system settings or uninstall the app.

For data processed by external analytics or advertising services, the scope of deletion may depend on whether the data can be linked to a specific app installation or technical identifier.
